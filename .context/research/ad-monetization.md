# Ad Revenue Research: Feng Shui Calendar App (LATAM + Spain)

## eCPM Benchmarks (Android, 2025-2026)

### Latin America
| Format | eCPM Range |
|---|---|
| Banner (320x50) | $0.15–$0.50 |
| Interstitial | $1.50–$3.67 |
| Rewarded Video | $2.00–$4.00 |

Country blended eCPM: Mexico ~$0.76, Colombia ~$0.61, Brazil ~$0.53, Argentina ~$0.35

### Spain
| Format | eCPM Range |
|---|---|
| Banner | $0.40–$0.80 |
| Interstitial | $3.00–$6.00 |
Spain is ~3-5x more valuable per impression than LATAM average.

## Revenue Projections

Assumes: 1 session/day, ~90sec, 70% fill rate

### Banner Only
| DAU | Monthly Revenue |
|---|---|
| 1,000 | ~$22 |
| 5,000 | ~$110 |
| 10,000 | ~$220 |
| 50,000 | ~$1,100 |
| 100,000 | ~$2,200 |

### Banner + 1 Interstitial
| DAU | Monthly Revenue |
|---|---|
| 1,000 | ~$75 |
| 5,000 | ~$373 |
| 10,000 | ~$745 |
| 50,000 | ~$3,725 |
| 100,000 | ~$7,450 |

## Break-Even (lean solo project ~$150/month)
- Banner only: ~6,800 DAU
- Banner + interstitial: ~2,000 DAU

## Recommendations
- **Start with**: AdMob banner (bottom) + "remove ads" IAP ($1.99)
- **Consider**: Exit interstitial (test retention impact first)
- **Ad network**: AdMob primary + Meta Audience Network as mediation
- **Placement**: Sticky bottom banner, below content, subtle separator
