# Competitive Research: Chinese Feng Shui Calendar Apps

**Date:** February 2026
**Focus:** Competitive landscape with emphasis on the Spanish-speaking market gap

---

## Reference App: Feng Shui Calendar Widget (SKY HORSE LLC)

- **Platform:** iOS only (17.6+), iPhone, iPad, Mac, Vision Pro
- **Pricing:** Free + $2.99/mo or $9.99/yr
- **Rating:** 5.0/5 (4 ratings — very new)
- **Language:** English only
- **Features:** Daily auspicious/inauspicious, solar terms, wisdom quotes, home screen widget, tear-off design
- **No Android, no Spanish**

### Android
- Feng Shui Fortune Calendar — English only, last updated Apr 2025
- 万年历 / Huangli — Chinese only, dominant in Chinese diaspora
- Almanac Chinese Lunar Calendar — English, covers 1901–2099

### Key Gaps
- **No Spanish-language Huangli/almanac app exists** — only generic zodiac apps
- No minimalist "tear-off calendar" design except SKY HORSE
- Most apps are data-dense and utility-focused, not lifestyle/ritual
- Widget quality is generally poor across the category

## Spanish-Speaking Market

### Existing Spanish content (web only, no apps)
- **Feng Shui Mundo** (Argentina) — weekly Chinese horoscope, courses, TikTok/Instagram presence
- **Leyda Ramírez** (Venezuela) — sells monthly Tong Shu PDF ($30/year), Feng Shui consultations
- **Horóscopo Chino apps** — Spanish zodiac-only apps, no almanac/Huangli

### Market signals
- Latin America astrology app revenue: **$205M in 2024** (5x growth from 2019)
- Global astrology app market: **$3.94B in 2024**, 20.5% CAGR
- Physical Chinese tear-off calendars sold on MercadoLibre Mexico/Colombia
- Android dominant platform in Latin America
- Feng Shui has genuine cultural traction (blended with local spiritual practices)

### Uncontested gap
No app delivers the daily Huangli almanac (do's/don'ts, solar terms, proverbs) in Spanish. Spanish-speaking Feng Shui practitioners have audiences but no app to recommend.

---

## Our Differentiators
1. **First Spanish-language Huangli app** — uncontested niche
2. **Design-first, lifestyle positioning** — daily ritual, not reference tool
3. **Android-first** — matches Latin America's dominant platform
4. **Bilingual (Spanish + Chinese characters)** — cultural authenticity
5. **Creator ecosystem** — existing Spanish Feng Shui influencers as distribution channel
