# Chinese Feng Shui Calendar App

## What
A tear-off calendar app inspired by the traditional Chinese Feng Shui Huangli almanac. Daily view showing auspicious activities, things to avoid, solar terms, and Chinese wisdom one-liners. Clean, modern minimalist design.

## Platform
- Mobile app: Android first, iOS later
- Home screen widget planned

## Status
- Early stage: defining architecture and tech stack
